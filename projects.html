Featured Project

AR Magazine for Disabilities

Accessible Digital Magazine Platform

Problem

Many older adults and people with disabilities struggle to access traditional digital news content due to visual, physical, or cognitive challenges.

Solution

Designed and implemented an accessible AR-based digital magazine platform that delivers news content through audio and video, improving ease of use and engagement for users with disabilities.

Key Contributions

  • Developed features supporting audio and video news delivery.
  • Focused on intuitive, user-friendly interface design.
  • Ensured accessibility by tailoring features to user needs.
  • Applied user-centric principles during design and testing.

Skills & Technologies

  • Problem analysis and solution design
  • Basics of Python / Java for logic and automation components
  • Accessibility and usability concepts
  • User-focused requirement gathering
